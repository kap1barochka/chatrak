About Us The Chatrak Legacy

What's Make Our Game Special!

Chatrak started with a deep passion for chess not just as a game, but as the highest form of craftsmanship. We believe that every match deserves a proper setting, and every piece is a small sculpture with its own soul.

Today, our collection includes everything from strict Staunton tournament sets to exotic elm burl and ebony boards brought from all over the world.
